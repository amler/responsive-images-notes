Using img with srcset & sizes

The new srcset attribute (of the img element) is a hint to the browser. It suggests to the browser that it should load one image on a small low-res screen, and load another in a big, high-res one. However, the browser can still choose to ignore this suggestion. For instance, if the browser knows the network conditions are poor, or the device is running out of battery, it may choose to download a low resolution image even to an HD Tablet.
